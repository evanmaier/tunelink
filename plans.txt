Application Structure:

USER
    name
    picture
    location
    instruments
    rating

INSTRUMENT
    make
    model
    year
    condition
    price

Users can search for available instruments in their area.
Search filters can include make, model, price, etc...
Once a user requests to rent an instrument, the owner can approve the rental.
If the owner approves, payment is processed and the instrument is exchanged.
owners can rate renters and vice verca.
